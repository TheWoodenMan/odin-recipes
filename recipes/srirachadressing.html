I like salad, especially spinach but it can be a little bland. Well this little mixture will definitely wake up your tastebuds

Works well with salads including cold meats or sausages too.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ tbsp Seasoned rice vinegar
  • (or rice wine vinegar is fine too)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil, Extra Virgin
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha hot sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • ½ tsp Ground black pepper
  • Red or white wine to taste

take care to stir clockwise now, this recipe can be tricky!

Method:

  1. Pour yourself a nice glass of wine
  2. Mix all the other ingredients in a different glass
  3. Add to the salad and toss untl coated

Key Point: The type of Sriracha you use does actually have an impact, go with Thai. I use flying goose but any authentic brand will work.

Key Point: Don't substitute with other types of hot sauces. It is thicker in constistency with less chilli, more garlic and sugar.

Wine Suggestions

When you make a salad with this stuff, it has the potential to overpower many wines, so go with something that will either soothe that or alternatively - match it's power

  • Alvarinho or Vinho Verde
  • Moscato
  • New world shiraz 14%+
  • Chilean Merlot
